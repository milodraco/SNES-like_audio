spx_aft.bin          = amiga frequency table (period lookup)
spx_aftf.bin         = amiga frequency table (frequency lookup)
spx_lft.bin          = linear frequency table (frequency lookup)
spx_core.bin         = xmsnes spc driver
spx_core.bin_real_hw = modified driver for spc output

rename the spx_core.bin_real_hw to spx_core.bin if you are going to
use the tune in your snes prod on real hardware.
